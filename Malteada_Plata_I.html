Banana milk shake

Ingredients:

Bananas Foster style:

4 tablespoons butter without salt

2 ripe bananas, peeled, cut into slices 1cm thick

1/2 cup of brown sugar

1/4 teaspoon of cinnamon

1/4 cup of banana liqueur

1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

1/2 teaspoon salt

Milk shake:

237ml of Jamaican dark rum, like Myers

4 cups vanilla ice cream, lightly thawed

1 cup whipped cream

A pinch of cinnamon

4 maraschino cherries, with stem

Preparation:

For Foster style bananas:

Put butter and bananas in a medium nonstick skillet over medium heat. Cook until the bananas are caramelized, about 2 minutes per side. Add brown sugar and cinnamon and stir.

Pulling the pan against the fire, add the banana liqueur and mix to combine. Add vanilla extract and salt, and stir. Put the Foster-style bananas in a bowl and let them cool.

Combine rum, chilled Foster bananas and ice cream in a blender. Blend until smooth and creamy. Pour into glasses and cover with a spoonful of whipped cream. Sprinkle with cinnamon and decorate with cherry. Serve with a long cigarette.
