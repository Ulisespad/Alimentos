Blackberry with Strawberry milk shake

Ingredients:

500 grams of Fresh strawberry

500 grams of fresh blackberries

50 grams of Brown sugar

500 milliliters of whole milk

Preparation:

In a bowl add the blackberries and strawberries without stem. Process everything with the help of a mixer.

Add milk and process.

Finally, add the brown sugar and rectify the flavor if you want to add more sugar. You can also sweeten the drink with a little honey.

Serve the strawberry and blackberry milkshake decorating each of the glasses with a blackberry and a strawberry. Enjoy.
